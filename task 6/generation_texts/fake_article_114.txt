A question that has been asked by numerous studies is whether and to what extent the Arabidopsis genes can be used to control the growth and development of Arabidopsis.

Although the Arabidopsis genome contains a number of Arabidopsis genes, the Arabidopsis genome is not an exception. These Arabidopsis genes have been expressed in diverse Arabidopsis species. On the basis of their binding to a receptor family, the Arabidopsis genes have been characterized as being expressed in the Arabidopsis cell bodies which are present in all Arabidopsis species.
